About us

Our research seeks a predictive understanding of biomolecular dynamics and mechanisms that underlie biological function and shape evolution.

We are bringing together data science / deep learning -- a 10,000-ft view of all known data and patterns -- and NMR spectroscopy -- an intimate view of biomolecules' motions and timing in ambient conditions.

We are in UW-Madison Biochemistry, affiliated with Biostatistics and Medical Informatics, and friends with NMRFAM.
