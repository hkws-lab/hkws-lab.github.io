About us

Our research seeks a predictive understanding of biomolecular dynamics and mechanisms that underlie biological function and shape evolution.

We are bringing together data science, deep learning, and NMR spectroscopy. Deep learning: a 10,000-ft view of all known data and patterns. NMR: an intimate view of biomolecules' motions and timing.

We are based at UW-Madison https://biochem.wisc.edu/ affiliated with https://biostat.wiscweb.wisc.edu/, and friends with https://nmrfam.wisc.edu/.
